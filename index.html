Project Scope

Job Titles: Event Coordinator, Project Manager, Fundraising Coordinator, Nonprofit Program Coordinator, Finance Assistant

Description:
Organize a charity fundraiser event to support a local cause. The event will include activities such as a silent auction, a bake sale, live music, and games for children.

Objectives:

  • Raise funds for the local charity.
  • Engage the community and increase awareness about the charity's mission.
  • Provide a fun and enjoyable experience for attendees.

Duration:
Planning and preparation: 2 months
Event duration: 1 day
